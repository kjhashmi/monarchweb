Case Study: Crown Restoration & Natural Hairline Redesign After a Previous Transplant

Patient: Gary
Clinic: Monarch Hair Transplant Clinic

🔍 Background

Gary approached Monarch Hair Transplant Clinic after being left disappointed by a previous hair transplant performed elsewhere. While his initial procedure focused solely on the frontal hairline, the results didn't align with his expectations — both in style and density. His crown remained untouched and continued to thin, and the existing hairline lacked the natural flow and shape he had hoped for.

🧠 Key Concerns:

  • Persistent diffuse thinning in the crown, an area known to be technically challenging
  • A flat, unnatural hairline that didn't complement his features
  • Low confidence following a previous underwhelming result
  • Desire for a refined, fuller look across both the crown and frontal zone

🛠 Our Approach

  • Redesigned the hairline with a soft, broken, and natural pattern that better suited his face and age
  • Densified the crown, carefully following the native hair direction and whorl to restore natural texture and appearance
  • Blended all transplanted areas into existing hair to create a seamless, undetectable finish

This involved meticulous planning to maximize existing donor resources and correct previous aesthetic shortcomings, all while achieving a balanced, realistic result.

🌟 Outcome

Gary's results speak for themselves. His crown now shows strong, even coverage — a significant transformation from the visible thinning he previously lived with. The new hairline not only looks natural but is stylistically on point, offering him flexibility and confidence.
