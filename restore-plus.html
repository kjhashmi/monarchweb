Medical-Grade Hair Regrowth Treatment Backed by Clinical Expertise

Monarch Restore+ is our signature, clinic-developed solution for men and women experiencing hair thinning, receding hairlines, or general pattern hair loss. Created by the specialist team at Monarch Hair Transplant Clinic, this advanced formula is part of a complete medical approach to hair restoration — available exclusively to patients following a face-to-face or virtual consultation with our doctors.

Unlike off-the-shelf options, Monarch Restore+ is offered with ongoing clinical oversight and can be tailored to your unique needs based on your consultation. Whether you're at the early stages of hair loss or looking to maintain results post-transplant, Restore+ is a key part of a personalised treatment plan designed to deliver visible, sustainable results.

What Makes Monarch Restore+ Different?

Doctor-Prescribed Treatment

Monarch Restore+ is available only after a full medical assessment to ensure suitability, safety, and optimal dosing for your hair loss type.

Tailored Formulation

Our solutions can be compounded to suit your needs — whether that's a stronger topical blend, a lower systemic dose, or a combination approach.

UK-Regulated Supply Chain

All medication is dispensed via UK-registered partner pharmacies, ensuring safety, compliance, and discretion.

Full Clinical Support

You'll be supported by our experienced medical team, with optional follow-up consultations to monitor your progress and adjust the plan as needed.

Who Is It For?

Monarch Restore+ is ideal for:

  • Men experiencing early to moderate male pattern baldness
  • Women dealing with diffuse thinning or stress-related hair shedding
  • Post-transplant patients looking to maintain and enhance results
  • Anyone seeking a medical, clinically monitored alternative to online-only hair loss brands

Safe. Private. Effective.

At Monarch, we believe hair restoration should never be one-size-fits-all. That's why every patient receives a private, professional consultation before beginning treatment. From your first appointment to long-term support, you'll receive expert care at every step.

Discreet UK Delivery

After your consultation, your personalised treatment can be shipped directly to your door with full clinical instructions and support.
