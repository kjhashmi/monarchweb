FUE Hair Transplant

Precision Focused. Natural Appearance. Expert Care.

At Monarch Hair Transplant Clinic, we use Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE), a highly refined method for hair restoration. It involves the careful harvesting of individual follicular units from donor areas and implanting them directly into thinning or bald regions—designed to deliver results that appear entirely natural.

What You Should Know

  • • Individual follicles are relocated—using proprietary tools—to replicate your natural hair growth pattern.
  • • No linear scar forms, and noticeable recovery time is kept to a minimum.
  • • Most patients are comfortable returning to daily routines within a few days.

Why Patients Choose FUE at Monarch

  • • Artfully Designed Hairlines – Crafted for natural symmetry and flow
  • • Advanced Expertise – Performed by surgeons trained to high CPSA and CQC standards
  • • Personalised Treatment Plans – Based on thorough assessment of hair type, donor availability, and long-term goals
  • • Proven Safety Standards – Conducted in our CQC-registered facility, aligned with national hair restoration best practices

Who It Suits

  • • Experiencing male or female pattern hair loss
  • • Seeking restoration of hairlines, crowns, beards, or brows
  • • Who prefer to avoid visible scarring from other methods
  • • With various hair types—straight, wavy, Afro-textured

The FUE Journey at Monarch

  1. Consultation & Assessment: We evaluate your scalp, donor capacity, and goals.
  2. Procedure Day: Small grafts are placed individually—typically taking 6–10 hours, depending on graft count.
  3. Recovery & Care: Expect gentle redness or swelling; most resume normal activities within a few days.
  4. Growth Timeline: New hair growth begins after a few months, with fuller results by 6–12 months.
